Why invest in blockchain based assets?

Blockchain, the technology, has shown that information can be recorded even without trusting a certain organization, like banks. That changes everything. Almost every important - money, laws, rights, ownership - is nothing but just a recorded information.

What is Authorito token?

Authorito is an ERC20 token that represents your ownership in the fund itself. The value of Authorito token is always backed by the Net Asset Value (NAV) of the fund, therefore, always guaranteeing a minimum value of your token. Token holders are rewarded for holding the tokens. The longer you hold, better the rewards will be. Authorito Mining.

How are we securing the funds?

Crypto assets wallets are great for day-to-day spending, but storing large amounts for the long term requires extra security. So, we've developed authorito vault which keeps your crypto assets safe.

Every fund trades. What makes Authorito different?

You're right. Everyone trades their current assets to acquire a new asset. Crypto has a unique property that they can be brought into existence out of thin air by contributing to their blockchain. We use that too.
